Problems Addressed

We have developed an applicacation "Covi-Cure" that aims to solve the following problems:

  • Oxygen Crisis
  • Medicines/Vaccines Wastage
  • Ambulance shortage
  • Covid wards/ICU shortage

    • Vision

    Our vision is to not just be dependent on government and blame them if they fail, rather fighting collectively and winning this battle
