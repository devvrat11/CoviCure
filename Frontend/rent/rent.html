Lack of medical Infrastrucuture

Problem

Lack of wards and ICU setup has never taken this many lives. We can complain regarding lack of Infrastructural setup all the time but it solves no purpose as it is not practically possible to build new hospitals in a time where disease spread is at its peak.

Solution

Our Solution to this problem is simple but highly effective in not just long terms but short terms as well. As mentioned in the ambulance page, we can utilize resources of schools, colleges, etc totrat patients but another way of solving this problem is taking help from public. Interested people can rent their places and convert those into covid wards/ICUs which can reduce the burden from hospitals.
  • User registers/signs in
  • Clicks rent button
  • Fills up a form with details like address, dimensions of space
  • Form is received and analysed
  • Organisation verifies the medicines as per their standard procedure and posts on the borrow wall
  • Needy ones can be isolated and treated

    • * In this way we can efficiently provide treatment to the needy people at affordable price
