Medicine/Vaccine Wastage

Problem

The problem of wastage of medicines is not new in our country but in this covid era we just can't afford such wastage.With advancement in tech we have now this privilage to be in touch with almost anyone but still we have falied to utilize the same.

Solution

Our Solution to this problem is simple but highly effective in not just long terms but short terms as well. We can motivate people to share and borrow medicines and from same platform.
  • User registers/signs in
  • Clicks share button
  • Fills up a form with details like expiry date, medicines etc
  • Form is received and analysed
  • Organisation verifies the medicines as per their standard procedure and posts on the borrow wall
  • Needy ones can request and get medicines for free once verified

    • * In this way we can efficiently provide medicines to the needy people at affordable price or even free of cost
