Oxygen Crisis

Problem

We have all seen the and unfortunately still suffering the loss pf lives in a large number due to lack of Oxygen but on deeply analysing we found out that the major problem was not the lack of oxygen rather the lack of supply and mismanagement. o2 Concentrators could have proved to be the game changers but due to high cost and low availabilty we faced problems of black marketing here.

Solution

Our Solution to this problem is simple but highly effective in not just long terms but short terms as well. The organisation(govt/private/etc) that controls all the concentrators supply in a area/city/state can use this application in following way:
  • User registers/signs in
  • Request for Concentrator
  • Fills up a form with details like medical prescription, O2 level, etc
  • Form is received and analysed
  • Organisation verifies the patient as per their standard procedure
  • Concentrator is alloted to the patient at his/her home via medical staff
  • Concentrator is taken back and alloted to some other patient once the current patient recovers

    • * In this way we can efficiently provide oxygen the needy people at affordable price or even free of cost
